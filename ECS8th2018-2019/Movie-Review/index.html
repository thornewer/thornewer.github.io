Movie Summary

"Call Me By Your Name" is a 2017 coming-of-age romantic drama film. It takes place in the summer of 1983. 17-year-old Elio Perlman is with his family at their 17th-century villa in Lombardy, Italy. Oliver, a doctoral student who's working as an intern for Elio's father, comes to stay with them. Elio and Oliver have little in common, but nonetheless Elio finds himself increasingly attracted to Oliver. They become more physically and emotionally intimate throughout the movie, and when it is time for Oliver to go back home, Elio is heartbroken. Months later during Hanukkah, Oliver calls Elio's family to tell them he is engaged. Oliver tells Elio he "remembers everything". After the call Elio stares into the fireplace, crying and reminiscing.
